What is Kaffarah?

Kaffarah is an expiation or atonement for certain sins or broken oaths in Islam. It involves acts of worship such as charity, fasting, or freeing slaves to seek Allah's forgiveness. Giving charity as Kaffarah not only fulfills religious obligations but also brings lasting rewards.

How to Fulfill Kaffarah

Kaffarah can be fulfilled through three options, in order of preference:

  1. Freeing a slave (if possible in modern times)
  2. Feeding 60 poor people (two meals each)
  3. Fasting for 60 consecutive days

Through Infaq Charity, you can fulfill your Kaffarah by feeding the poor or supporting charitable projects that benefit communities.
