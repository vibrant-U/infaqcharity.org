Welcome to Fidya Appeal

During Ramadan, fasting is a fundamental pillar of Islam. However, not everyone can observe this obligation due to health, age, or other reasons. Fidya allows you to fulfill your duty while helping those in need.

Understanding Fidya

Fidya is the compensation paid by Muslims who are unable to fast during Ramadan due to age, illness, pregnancy, or other valid reasons. It involves providing food or its monetary equivalent to those in need.

Who Needs Fidya?

  • The Elderly: Those over 70 years who find fasting difficult
  • The Sick: People with chronic illnesses or medical conditions
  • Pregnant & Nursing: Women in advanced pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Travelers: Those on journeys that make fasting impractical

Your Fidya in Action

Through Infaq Charity, your Fidya donation provides nutritious meals and essential support to vulnerable Muslims in Uganda who cannot fast, ensuring they too can participate in the blessings of Ramadan.
