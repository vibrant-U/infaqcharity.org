The Importance of Zakat

Zakat is the third pillar of Islam, obligatory purification of wealth that purifies both wealth and soul.

Who Pays Zakat?

Zakat becomes obligatory when wealth reaches the Nisab threshold and has been held for one lunar year.

How to Pay Zakat

Your Zakat is distributed according to Islamic guidelines to eligible recipients including orphans, widows, the poor, and those in need. In Uganda, your Zakat funds:

  • Provides nutritious food and education for orphaned children
  • Builds clean water wells serving entire communities
  • Supports healthcare and medical treatment
  • Funds educational materials and school fees
