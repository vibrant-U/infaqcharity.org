Understanding Zakat

What is Zakat?

Zakat is one of the Five Pillars of Islam, obligatory purification of wealth distributed to those in need.

When is Zakat Due?

Zakat becomes obligatory when wealth reaches the Nisab threshold and has been held for one lunar year.

Who Benefits?

Zakat is distributed to orphans, widows, the poor, and other eligible recipients as defined in Islamic law.

Why Give Zakat?

Zakat purifies wealth, promotes social welfare, and brings blessings and barakah to your remaining wealth.
