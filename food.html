Welcome to Food Aid Programs

Hunger affects millions in Uganda. Through our comprehensive food aid programs, we provide immediate relief and long-term solutions to combat malnutrition and food insecurity.

The Hunger Crisis in Uganda

Millions in Uganda face food insecurity due to poverty, drought, and displacement. Malnutrition affects children's growth, health, and ability to learn. Your support provides immediate relief and long-term nutrition solutions.

Our Food Programs

  • Daily Meal Programs: Nutritious school meals for children
  • Emergency Food Relief: Immediate aid during crises and famines
  • Nutrition Education: Teaching families about balanced diets
  • Garden Projects: Community gardens for sustainable food production
  • Maternal Nutrition: Support for pregnant women and nursing mothers

Impact of Food Aid

  • Prevents malnutrition and stunting in children
  • Improves school attendance and concentration
  • Strengthens immune systems against disease
  • Provides dignity and hope to families
  • Creates pathways out of poverty through nutrition
