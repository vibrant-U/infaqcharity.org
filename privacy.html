Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 28, 2025 (Kampala, Uganda)**

Infaq Charity Organization is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors and website visitors. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data in compliance with international best practices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to process donations, issue receipts, and communicate about our projects. This includes:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, postal address, and telephone number.
  • Financial Data: Donation amounts, payment method (though full payment card details are never stored by usâ€”they are handled by secure third-party processors like PayPal/Stripe).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via Google Analytics, used to improve our website).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for charitable purposes in accordance with Islamic principles of Amanah (trust) and transparency, including:

  • Processing and confirming your donations (Zakat, Sadaqah, Sadaqah Jariyah, etc.).
  • Providing legally required tax receipts and financial statements for Zakat compliance.
  • Sending updates on Islamic charitable projects you supported (unless you opt-out).
  • Maintaining donor records for internal Sharia audits and legal compliance.
  • Facilitating Zakat distribution according to Islamic guidelines and eligible categories.

3. Data Protection and Security

We maintain high security standards, especially critical as we handle international funds:

  • All online financial transactions are encrypted using SSL/TLS technology.
  • We utilize PCI-compliant third-party gateways for payment processing.
  • Access to donor databases is strictly limited to authorized Infaq staff in Uganda.

4. Sharing of Information (Donor Confidentiality)

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to outside parties. We may share data only with:

  • Payment Processors: Required to complete the transaction.
  • Legal Authorities: If legally mandated by Ugandan or international law (e.g., anti-money laundering checks).

5. Contacting Us About Your Data

If you have questions about this policy, or wish to review, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data, please contact our Data Compliance Officer:
