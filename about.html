Our Mission

To implement sustainable, impactful projects across Uganda that address poverty, illiteracy, and water scarcity, guided by Islamic principles of Infaq (spending in the way of Allah) and social justice.

Our Vision

A future where every community in Uganda has access to clean water, quality education, stable infrastructure (Masjid/Schools), and sustained support for vulnerable populations, particularly orphans.
