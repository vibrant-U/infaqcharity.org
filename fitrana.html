Welcome to Fitrana Appeal

Fitrana (Zakat al-Fitr) is an obligatory charity given at the end of Ramadan before Eid al-Fitr prayer. It purifies the fasting person from any shortcomings and helps the poor celebrate Eid. "The fasting person will have his sins forgiven by Allah." (Sahih al-Bukhari)

The Blessings of Fitrana

Fitrana purifies your fasting, expiates sins, and brings joy to needy families during Eid. It ensures that no Muslim goes hungry on the day of celebration and strengthens the bonds of brotherhood in the community.

Who Should Pay Fitrana?

Fitrana is obligatory for every Muslim who has enough food for himself and his family for one day and night during Eid al-Fitr.
