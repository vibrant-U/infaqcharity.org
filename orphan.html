Welcome to Orphan Sponsorship

"And they give food, despite their own need, to the orphan and the needy." (Quran 76:8) Many children in Uganda have lost their parents due to poverty, disease, or conflict. Your sponsorship ensures they receive the support they need to thrive.

Why Sponsor an Orphan?

Your sponsorship provides essential support to ensure orphaned children receive education, food, healthcare, and emotional care, giving them a chance at a brighter future.

What Your Sponsorship Covers

  • School fees and supplies
  • Nutritious meals
  • Medical care and vaccinations
  • Clothing and basic needs
  • Mentorship and counseling
