Welcome to Qurban Appeal

Qurban (Udhiyah) is the sacrificial act performed during Eid al-Adha, commemorating Prophet Ibrahim's willingness to sacrifice his son for Allah. "And for every community We have appointed a rite of sacrifice." (Quran 22:34)

The Significance of Qurban

Qurban is a beloved act of worship that demonstrates our submission to Allah and our compassion for those in need. The meat from your sacrifice reaches families who may not afford to celebrate Eid properly.

Who Should Perform Qurban?

Qurban is obligatory for every Muslim who is sane, adult, free, and possesses wealth above the Nisab threshold on the days of Eid al-Adha.
